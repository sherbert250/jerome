Me

My name is Jerome Trayer and I am currently a 22 year old student and software developer. I am currently a triple major at Arizona State University studying statistics, economics, and computer science. I also am working part time as a software developer for State Farm Insurance. In my free time, I enjoy solving my Rubik's Cubes, playing Super Smash Brothers Melee, and having a good night out with my friends. I'm also very passionate about tutoring and hope to one day become a professor that simultaneously works in the industry. Right now, software development is my passion. I say 'right now' because I had never even seen a line of code until I was 19. I fell in love so quickly that I picked up my third major in school and did everything I could to improve my coding skills. I've working on everything from Raspberry PIs and Arduinos to highly available enterprise web applications and services. My addiction though is writing scripts with JQuery and JavaScript to automate anything I can do in a browser. You can check out my work in the portfolio page to see what I'm working on.
